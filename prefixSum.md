# 如何初始化二维数组的前缀和

```text
+---+---+---+        +---+---+      +---+---+---+      +---+---+ 
| 1 | 2 | 3 |        | 1 | 2 |      | 1 | 2 | 3 |      | 1 | 2 |
+---+---+---+        +---+---+      +---+---+---+      +---+---+
| 4 | 5 | 6 |        | 4 | 5 |      | 4 | 5 | 6 |      | 4 | 5 |
+---+---+---+        +---+---+.     +---+---+---+      +---+---+        +---+
| 7 | 8 | 9 |        | 7 | 8 |                                          | 9 |
+---+---+---+    =   +---+---+   +                 -               +    +---+
```

# 如何计算任意子矩阵的元素和

```text
               +---+---+---+       +---+       +---+---+---+     +---+
               | 1 | 2 | 3 |       | 1 |       | 1 | 2 | 3 |     | 1 |
+---+---+      +---+---+---+       +---+       +---+---+---+     +---+
| 5 | 6 |      | 4 | 5 | 6 |       | 4 | 
+---+---+      +---+---+---+       +---+
| 8 | 9 |      | 7 | 8 | 9 |       | 7 | 
+---+---+  =   +---+---+---+   -   +---+   -                  + 
```

用python的 accumulate 方法构建前缀和

```python
from itertools import accumulate

nums = [1, 2, 3, 4]
prefix_sum = list(accumulate(nums)) # 计算前缀和
print(prefix_sum) # 输出: [1, 3, 6, 10]
```
